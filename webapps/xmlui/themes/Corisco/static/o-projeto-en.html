Paulo Freire Memory and Presence: preservation and democratization of access to the Brazilian cultural heritage

With the sponsorship of Petrobras through the Lei Federal de Incentivo à Cultura, the Paulo Freire Institute launched the project "Paulo Freire Memória e Presença: preservação e democratização do acesso ao patrimônio cultural brasileiro". The project was contemplated in a public selection process in the Programa Petrobras Cultural, 2010 edition, it realizes this and other Paulo Freire's and Freirean national and international community's dreams.

General Goals

Preservar os materiais existentes, garantir e democratizar acesso qualificado à obra e ao legado de Paulo Freire, inclusive a pessoas com deficiência visualerve the materials, ensure and democratize access for all to the Paulo Freire's work and legacy, including people with visual disabilities.

Specific Goals

  • Organize and catalog the archives of the Reference Center Paulo Freire.
  • Scan thirty thousand pages of text, a hundred videos, two hundred tapes, five hundred photos produced by Paulo Freire or presenting him, and provide two thousand pages of and about Paulo Freire for people with visual impairments, from the perspective of social inclusion.
  • Scan twenty thousand pages of text, a hundred videos and two thousand five hundred photos produced by Paulo Freire Institute, as reinvention of the legacy.
  • Provide all the digitalized collection of the Reference Center Paulo Freire on the network also for consultation at the Institute.

Public Beneficiary

Paulo Freire's work is available in the building of the Institute situated in Sao Paulo. Given its importance, it is urgent and necessary its digitalization to preserve and enhance the access to the materials provided in the Reference Center Paulo Freire, in a physical or virtual format, widening the gateway to library users, information providers, researchers from all knowledge areas, Brazilians and foreigners. Paulo Freire's work and legacy falls within that movement for the democratization of access to knowledge.

In this context, it is necessary to publicize the contribution of Paulo Freire's life and work, that is an international cultural heritage, expanding his literature access to universities, social movements, people and organizations around the world. In addition there will be a contribution for teachers trainning, popular educators and all those interested in educational processes in an inter-transdisciplinary emancipatory perspective.
